 Get to know Josh and find out why he loves cross-country mountain biking
 
 
 
 Learning to ride
 
 Josh started riding about 6 years ago. A friend convinced him to try it and he’s been hooked ever since.

“It was a bit hard to convince myself to go downhill at first, but when I started to trust the bike and the tires, I realized that I’d found a super-cool new hobby”
 
 
 An outdoor lifestyle
 
Josh goes camping and plays ultimate frisbee, but riding his bike is what he’s truly passionate about.

“There’s something special about the feeling of loose dirt under your tires… it’s hard to describe, but I absolutely love it”
 
  Top speed: 65 kph
 
 Want to know why someone rides a bike down the side of a mountain? For Josh, it’s about adrenaline, speed, and a sense of accomplishment.
